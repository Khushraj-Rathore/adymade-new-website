How It Works

Brief to Published in 5 Clear Steps

A structured, transparent process so you know exactly what is happening at every stage — and nothing goes live without your approval.

1

Brief & Scope

You fill a short brief form: your brand, message, target audience, preferred style, and any reference videos. Takes 10 minutes. No meeting required to get started.

2

Script & Storyboard

We write the full script and a visual storyboard describing each scene. You review and suggest changes. Nothing moves to production until you sign off on this stage.

3

AI Production

Our production pipeline generates visuals, voiceover, motion graphics, sound design, and music — all in parallel. This stage happens entirely within our team, with no need for your time.

4

Review & Refine

You receive a draft within the agreed window. Watch it, leave timestamped feedback, and we refine. We iterate until the video matches your vision — no revision limit.

5

Delivery

Final files delivered via Google Drive or WeTransfer in all required formats: MP4 for publishing, high-resolution exports, platform-specific aspect ratios, and source files on request.
