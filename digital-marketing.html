What Is Digital Marketing

Every Rupee Should Bring Back More Than One

Digital marketing is the full stack of online channels used to attract, engage, and convert customers — Google search ads, Meta social ads, organic social media content, email sequences, and WhatsApp broadcasts. When done well, it compounds: each channel reinforces the others and the cost per acquisition falls over time.

Most small businesses either do too little (posting occasionally on Instagram and calling it marketing) or too much in the wrong places (running ads before the website converts). We audit what you have, identify the highest-leverage channels for your specific business, and build a focused strategy that generates measurable returns.

We manage campaigns in-house — no outsourcing, no white-labelled dashboards, no account managers who have never opened your ad account. Every campaign is owned, monitored, and optimised by our team. You speak directly to the person running your ads.

Key Features

What Makes Our Marketing Different from the Rest

Most agencies optimise for looking busy. We optimise for your cost per acquisition and revenue generated. These are the practices that separate performance marketing from theatre.

Revenue-Focused Reporting

We report on leads, cost per lead, conversion rate, and revenue attribution — not impressions and reach. Every monthly report shows what the spend generated in business value, not just traffic.

Full-Funnel Strategy

Top-of-funnel awareness, mid-funnel engagement, and bottom-of-funnel conversion — each stage has distinct creative and bidding strategies. We do not run one ad to everyone and call it a campaign.

Audience Segmentation

Retargeting website visitors. Lookalike audiences based on your existing clients. Exclusion lists to stop wasting spend on people who already converted. Audience strategy is where the ROI lives.

Continuous A/B Testing

Headlines, creatives, landing pages, CTAs — we run structured tests at every stage of the funnel. Data replaces opinion. Every ad account improves with every cycle. We never let an account go stale.

WhatsApp Marketing

India-specific advantage: WhatsApp Business API broadcasts for lead nurturing, cart recovery, appointment reminders, and promotional campaigns. 95%+ open rates vs 20% for email — this channel is underused by most Indian businesses.

No Lock-In Monthly Plans

Month-to-month retainers with 30-day notice. We earn your business every month by delivering results. We do not hold your ad accounts or creative assets hostage. If you leave, you keep everything we built.

How It Works

Audit to Growth in 4 Clear Steps

A structured onboarding that turns the chaos of existing marketing spend into a focused, measurable programme in the first month.

1

Marketing Audit

We review your current ad accounts, website analytics, conversion tracking, and competitor landscape. We identify where spend is being wasted, which channels are performing, and what is missing entirely.

2

Strategy & Goals

We define target CPL (cost per lead), target ROAS (return on ad spend), monthly budget allocation across channels, audience segmentation, and a 90-day content and campaign calendar. Agreed in writing before we start.

3

Launch & Execute

Campaigns go live. Creatives produced, audiences built, tracking verified, bidding strategies set. The first two weeks are observation — we let data accumulate before making significant changes.

4

Optimise & Report

Weekly micro-optimisations (bids, audiences, placements) and a monthly performance report with revenue attribution, recommendations, and next month's plan. You review and approve every major strategy change.
