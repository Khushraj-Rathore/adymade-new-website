Key Features

Every Website We Build Ships With These

Non-negotiable standards that are included in every project — not optional extras or add-ons you need to ask for.

Sub-3 Second Load Time

Compressed assets, lazy loading, CDN delivery, and modern image formats ensure every site scores 90+ on Google PageSpeed. Slow websites lose clients — we do not build slow websites.

Mobile-First Design

Every layout is designed and tested on mobile first, then scaled up. With 70%+ of web traffic now on mobile, this is not optional. Every tap target, every font, every image is optimised for small screens.

SEO-Ready from Day One

Semantic HTML, correct heading hierarchy, meta tags, Open Graph, JSON-LD schema, XML sitemap, and robots.txt — all configured correctly from the start. No SEO retrofitting needed after launch.

SSL, Security & Uptime

HTTPS enforced, security headers configured, dependencies kept updated. We recommend and set up hosting with 99.9% uptime SLA and daily backups — your site is always on and always safe.

Self-Managed CMS

Most clients want to update their own content without calling a developer. We build with WordPress, Webflow, or custom CMS so your team can edit pages, add blog posts, and update services independently.

Analytics & Conversion Tracking

Google Analytics 4, Google Search Console, Meta Pixel, and conversion event tracking configured on launch. You know exactly which pages drive enquiries and where visitors drop off — from day one.

How It Works

Brief to Launch in 5 Clear Steps

A structured process that gives you full visibility at every stage — no disappearing acts, no scope creep, no surprises at handover.

1

Discovery & Scope

We document your goals, target audience, pages required, integrations, and timeline. You receive a fixed-price proposal within 24 hours of the brief.

2

Design & Wireframes

We create page layouts and design mockups for your approval before writing a line of code. Your visual feedback at this stage shapes the build.

3

Development

We build the site with clean, semantic code. You receive a staging link and can track progress live — no black-box development behind closed doors.

4

Review & Refine

You test the staging site, provide feedback, and we make revisions. Multiple rounds included. Nothing goes live until you are satisfied with every detail.

5

Launch & Handover

We deploy to your domain, configure DNS, set up Analytics and Search Console, and hand over full access. A 30-day support window covers any post-launch issues.
