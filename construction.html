What We Do for Construction

The Full Digital Stack for Construction Companies Operating in the GCC

Construction companies in Saudi Arabia, UAE, and the Gulf face a specific combination of challenges: multilingual workforces, strict HSE audit requirements, complex equipment tracking and billing, and the need for a credible digital presence to win large tenders. Generic digital agencies understand none of these.

We have built the website for RGS — a scaffolding company under Tamimi Group, one of Saudi Arabia's largest conglomerates — and understand the specific requirements of GCC construction clients firsthand. We know what a Tier 1 Saudi client expects from a supplier's online presence, how OSHA and Saudi Aramco HSE standards are applied, and what scaffold tracking and billing actually looks like operationally.

From site induction videos for a mixed-nationality workforce to a custom scaffold management system with cubic metre per day billing and client-facing reports — we build exactly what construction businesses need, not a generic digital package with a hard hat on it.
