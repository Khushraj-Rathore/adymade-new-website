Our Story

Built in Indore.
Operating Globally.

Adymade was founded with a straightforward belief: that Indian businesses — at every size and in every sector — deserve the same quality of digital execution that was previously available only to companies with the budget to hire large agencies in Mumbai, Delhi, or abroad.

We started by building websites and running ad campaigns. As AI tools matured, we began integrating automation and AI into everything we touched — not as a trend to chase, but because it made the work faster, more repeatable, and more valuable for clients.

Today, Adymade operates as a full-stack digital and AI agency from Indore, serving clients from the dental clinic down the street to a scaffolding company under Tamimi Group in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. The team is small by design — every client works directly with the people building their project, not account managers who relay messages.

We are not a generalist agency that does everything for everyone. We go deep on AI automation, web development, compliance training videos, and growth marketing — and we do those things at a standard that holds up internationally.

Why Choose Us

Six Reasons Clients Stay With Adymade

Not the reasons we invented for a pitch deck — the reasons clients actually come back and refer others.

You Talk to the Builder

No account managers relaying messages between you and an invisible team. The person who answers your WhatsApp is the person building your project. Decisions get made in one conversation, not four.

Fixed Price, Every Time

Every project gets a fixed-price proposal before work begins. We do not bill hourly, we do not add scope creep charges, and the price you agree to is the price you pay. No surprises on the final invoice.

AI-First, Not AI-Adjacent

We do not bolt AI onto a traditional agency model. Our workflows, our tools, and our thinking are AI-native. That is why we can deliver at a speed and price point that traditional agencies structurally cannot match.

Real GCC Market Knowledge

Not theoretical. We have built for the GCC — bilingual Arabic websites with RTL layout, OSHA and Saudi Aramco HSE-aligned compliance videos, and scaffold management software for the Gulf billing model. We know what Tier 1 Saudi clients expect.

You Own Everything

Code, creative assets, ad accounts, domain, analytics — all of it belongs to you from day one. We do not hold assets hostage. If you leave tomorrow, you take everything we built for you with you. No platform lock-in.

GEO — First Mover Advantage

We are one of the first Indian agencies offering Generative Engine Optimisation — getting your business cited in ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews. Most competitors have not started. The window is open now.
