What Is GEO

The Next Frontier of Search — Already Here

When someone types a question into ChatGPT or Perplexity, the AI does not show a list of blue links. It reads across thousands of sources, synthesises an answer, and — if it trusts your content — cites your business directly in that answer. This is GEO: the practice of optimising your online presence so AI engines trust, understand, and recommend your business.

Traditional SEO optimises for Google's ranking algorithm. GEO optimises for AI understanding — structured data, entity recognition, direct-answer content, and authoritative citations. These are different disciplines requiring different strategies.

Key Features

Seven Signals That Make AI Engines Trust You

GEO is not one tactic — it is a combination of content structure, technical signals, and authority building that collectively tells AI engines your business is the right answer to recommend.

Schema Markup & Structured Data

JSON-LD schema applied to every page: LocalBusiness, Service, FAQPage, BreadcrumbList, and AggregateRating. AI engines parse structured data to understand your entity, services, and credibility — without reading every word.

Direct-Answer Content Writing

Every service page opens with a crisp, direct answer to the question it targets. This is the format AI engines pull from when generating responses — we write content specifically structured to be cited verbatim.

FAQ Schema Optimisation

8 to 10 questions per page written exactly as your customers phrase them to ChatGPT or Perplexity, then marked up with FAQPage JSON-LD. This is the single highest-impact GEO tactic and appears in both Google rich results and AI answers.

Named Entity Optimisation

AI knowledge graphs are built from named entities. We ensure your content consistently mentions real clients, real tool names, real locations, and specific outcomes — the signals that establish your entity as authoritative and verifiable.

Authority Directory Listings

Clutch, GoodFirms, G2, Crunchbase, and LinkedIn — the sources AI engines specifically pull from when identifying service providers. We create, optimise, and maintain your presence across all AI-cited directories.

GEO Citation Monitoring

We run monthly checks across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Bing Copilot using your target queries — tracking whether your business appears, how it is described, and what needs to improve in the next cycle.

Frequently Asked

Questions About GEO

GEO (Generative Engine Optimisation) is the practice of optimising your online presence so that AI-powered search tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Bing Copilot recommend your business when answering user queries. Traditional SEO optimises content and links to rank in Google's list of blue links. GEO optimises structured data, entity signals, and direct-answer content so that AI engines trust your information enough to cite it in their generated responses. They require different strategies, but they work best together — GEO builds on a solid SEO foundation.
GEO results typically begin appearing within 6 to 12 weeks of full implementation, depending on your current online presence and how competitive your target queries are. The first wins usually come from less competitive, more specific queries — such as your city or industry combined with your service — before broader queries. Unlike traditional SEO, GEO does not depend on waiting for search engine re-crawling cycles; AI engines pull from current web content more dynamically, so high-quality optimisations can show results faster.
GEO does not replace SEO — it extends it. A strong SEO foundation (technical site health, page authority, quality backlinks) actually improves your GEO results, because AI engines trust established, well-structured websites more. We recommend running both in parallel. Many of our GEO implementations are done for clients who already have an SEO programme, adding the structured data, direct-answer content, and entity signals that SEO alone does not address. If you only have budget for one, we will tell you honestly which will have more impact for your specific situation.
