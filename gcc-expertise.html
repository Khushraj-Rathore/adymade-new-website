What We Deliver for GCC

Six Capabilities That Make Us GCC-Ready

Full RTL layout, proper Arabic font selection (Noto Kufi, IBM Plex Arabic), mirror-correct navigation, culturally appropriate visual hierarchy, and a clean EN/AR language toggle. Both versions independently SEO-optimised for Arabic and English search queries in the GCC.

GCC HSE Compliance Videos

GCC-Specific Questions

What GCC Clients Ask Us Most Often

We work with professional Arabic translators and native-Arabic reviewers for all website content, compliance video scripts, and tender documents. You can supply Arabic content if you have it — we will review it for readability and layout fit. If you need translation, we handle the full process: English source content is written or provided, translated by a professional with Gulf market experience, and reviewed for tone, formality, and technical accuracy before it goes into the layout. We do not use automated machine translation for any GCC client deliverable.
Our scripts are researched against the applicable standard for your specific project — OSHA 1926, NEBOSH International General Certificate, Saudi Aramco SAEP-367, ISO 45001, or the specific client HSE requirements that your main contractor imposes on subcontractors. We include on-screen clause references and provide a compliance source document listing every regulatory citation used. Before production begins, your HSE manager or the client's HSE representative reviews and approves the script. If a specific audit requires particular content, duration, language, or assessment component, we configure the module to meet that requirement before committing to production.
We invoice international clients in USD, SAR, AED, or GBP — whichever your finance team prefers. Payment is accepted via bank transfer (SWIFT), Wise, or Payoneer. We issue proper invoices with all required details for your accounts payable process. For larger projects, we typically structure payment as 50% on project kick-off and 50% on delivery — or in milestone-based stages for longer engagements. We can provide a commercial invoice, pro-forma invoice, or service agreement in whatever format your procurement or compliance process requires.
